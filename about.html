About ImageShrink

Part of the Finchub Free Tools Ecosystem

What we built

ImageShrink is a free image compressor, resizer, and format converter that runs entirely in your browser. There's no upload step, no account, and no daily limit — because the tool uses your own device's processing power instead of a server, there's no ongoing cost that would force us to add one.

Why we built it

Most image tools online either upload your files to a server, bury useful features behind a paywall, or add a watermark to free output. We wanted something that respects your privacy by design and stays fast no matter how many people are using it.

Part of a larger toolkit

ImageShrink is developed by Finchub Labs alongside other free tools, including TikSnapTok for downloading TikTok videos, and Finchub, a hub for financial calculators and utilities.
