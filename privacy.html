Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

How your images are handled

ImageShrink processes every image entirely inside your own browser using JavaScript's Canvas API. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers, never stored anywhere by us, and never seen by us. Closing or refreshing the tab discards the image completely.

What we do collect

Like most websites, we use standard analytics (page views, general location, device type) to understand traffic and improve the site. This data does not include the images you process, since those never leave your device.

Cookies and local storage

We may use browser storage to remember your preferences, such as your last-used quality setting, so the tool feels consistent between visits. This data stays on your device.

Advertising

This site displays ads through third-party ad networks, which may use cookies to show relevant ads. You can control ad personalization through your browser or ad network settings.

Third-party services

Fonts and a small PDF-generation library are loaded from public CDNs to keep the site fast. These requests do not include your images.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through our contact page.
